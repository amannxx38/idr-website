About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on developing professionals in digital, cyber and technology risk. Our mission is to prepare practitioners who can operate in regulated and high-consequence environments such as finance, government and critical infrastructure.

Through collaboration with academic partners and UK universities, IDR combines research-led insight with practical projects to bridge the gap between theory and real-world risk management.

Our Model

Academy

Training programs and bootcamps designed for students and professionals to build expertise in cyber, AI and digital risk.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing new frameworks, intellectual property and governance models for future digital and AI risks.

Advisory Services

Supporting organisations with implementation of security and risk frameworks such as NIST, ISO 27001 and NIS2.

Our Community

IDR supports students, early-career professionals and experienced practitioners who want to build careers in digital and cyber risk. We work closely with sectors such as financial services, technology and critical infrastructure to develop future-ready talent.
