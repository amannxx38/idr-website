# Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) – Homepage Design

## Overview

This project is a responsive homepage and logo design for the **Institute of Digital Risk (IDR)**, an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber, and AI risk.

The website explains IDR’s mission, services, and community while demonstrating modern web design principles.

## Features

* Responsive design (desktop & mobile)
* Sticky navigation bar
* Smooth scrolling navigation
* Hero section with call-to-action
* About section explaining IDR
* Service pillars (Academy, Innovation & Incubation, Advisory Services)
* Community section
* Contact / Register interest form
* Minimalist tech-style design

## Logo Design

The IDR logo uses a **cube-inspired geometric icon** representing structure, risk management, and resilience.

**Color palette**

* Orange – innovation and technology
* Black – authority and professionalism
* White – clarity and minimalism

Two variants are included:

* Icon + Text
* Icon Only (for favicon / mobile)

## Technologies Used

* HTML5 (semantic structure)
* CSS3 (Flexbox & Grid)
* Vanilla JavaScript
* Google Fonts
